FAQ

Good questions

How do you choose what to test first?

We score hypotheses using impact, confidence, and ease (PXL/ICE). We start with opportunities nearest to the money: PDP → cart → checkout, unless research shows a bigger upstream win.

What stack do you use for testing?

Whatever fits your site: VWO/Optimizely for robust setups, native Shopify or lightweight scripts for landing pages. QA and guardrails prevent flicker and maintain run rules.

How soon can we see lift?

Audits ship in ~2 weeks with quick wins. For programs, we aim for the first validated test within 2–4 weeks, depending on traffic and complexity.
