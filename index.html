FAQ

Questions?

How do you choose what to test first?

I score hypotheses using a widely-used proven framework that takes in account the most impactful variables any hypothesis can have. This ensure resources are not wasted in testing hypothesis that don't actually move the needle.

Can I get a quote on a project?

Yes, we can discuss a project scope, responsabilities and compensation via your preferred communication channel (e-mail or Telegram).

How soon can we see lift?

I’m committed to letting the data speak. That’s how we avoid revisiting a test 2, 3, or 6 months later and finding the result “changed” simply because it was interpreted incorrectly. Test length varies with traffic volume—anywhere from a few days to several weeks—with most running 2–3 weeks.
